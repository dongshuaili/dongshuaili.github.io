¡Hola! ¡Bienvenidos!

My name is Dongshuai Li, I’m a Postdoctoral Researcher at DTU Space.

Currently I am the Principle Investigator(PI) of the DFF Sapere Aude Project - Studying Lightning Processes with Neuromorphic Imaging on Microsecond Time Scales. The project mainly concerns the inner plasma physics of lightning.
